# End User Guide - Exam Readiness Tracker (ERT)

## Welcome!

The Exam Readiness Tracker helps you prepare for certification exams through adaptive learning. The app tracks your progress on each question and focuses your study on areas where you need improvement.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Sign Up

1. Visit the ERT Quiz App
2. Click "Create Account"
3. Enter your email and create a password (min 8 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character)
4. Click "Create Account"

### 2. Verify Your Email

1. Check your email for a message with subject "Verify your ERT Quiz App account"
2. Copy the 6-digit verification code
3. Enter the code on the verification screen
4. Click "Confirm"

### 3. Log In

1. Enter your email and password
2. Click "Sign In"
3. You'll be taken to the Dashboard

---

## Using the Dashboard

The Dashboard shows your progress across all exams and subdomains.

**What you'll see:**
- **Exam filter** - Select a specific exam or view all exams
- **Subdomain breakdown** - Progress by topic area
- **Progress columns:**
  - **New** - Questions you haven't attempted yet
  - **Right** - Questions you've answered correctly (but not mastered)
  - **Wrong** - Questions you've answered incorrectly
  - **Mastered** - Questions you've answered correctly 3+ times
  - **Total** - Total questions in that subdomain
  - **Progress %** - Percentage of mastered questions

**Tips:**
- Focus on subdomains with low progress percentages
- Review "Wrong" questions to understand your weak areas
- Questions marked as "Mastered" won't appear in future quizzes

## Understanding Progress States

Questions move through different states based on your performance:

1. **NEW** ‚Üí First time seeing this question
2. **WRONG** ‚Üí Answered incorrectly (resets to this state on any wrong answer)
3. **RIGHT** ‚Üí Answered correctly 1-2 times
4. **MASTERED** ‚Üí Answered correctly 3+ times consecutively

**Key Points:**
- Getting a question wrong resets it to "Wrong" state
- You must answer correctly 3+ times to master a question
- Mastered questions are excluded from future quizzes
- You can manually mark questions as mastered (see below)

---

## Mark as Mastered

If you're confident you know a question:

1. During a quiz, click "Mark as Mastered" button
2. Confirm the action
3. The question will be excluded from future quizzes

**Use this feature when:**
- You know the answer without hesitation
- You've seen this question many times
- You want to focus on harder questions

---

## Tips for Effective Study

1. **Start with "New" questions** - Build your knowledge base
2. **Review "Wrong" questions regularly** - Learn from mistakes
3. **Focus on low-progress subdomains** - Balance your knowledge
4. **Read explanations carefully** - Understand why answers are correct
5. **Take shorter quizzes frequently** - Better than long cramming sessions
6. **Track your progress over time** - Watch your mastery percentage grow

## Troubleshooting

### Can't log in
- Verify your email first (check spam folder)
- Reset password if forgotten
- Contact admin if issues persist

### Not receiving verification email
- Check spam/junk folder
- Wait a few minutes (emails can be delayed)
- Try signing up again with a different email

### Questions not loading
- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection
- Try logging out and back in

### Progress not updating
- Ensure you clicked "Submit Answer"
- Refresh the dashboard
- Contact admin if data is missing
